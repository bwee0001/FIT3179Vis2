Opals

Australia's National Gemstone

___________________________________________________

Opal or the fire of the desert as known to the Indigenous people, was proclaimed as Australia's national gemstone on 23 July 1993. Australia's Opal fields alone are larger than the rest of the world's Opal fields combined.

There are 3 main forms of opals that are found in Australia, Black Opals, Boulder Opals and White or Milky opals. Of the three, Black Opals is the most valuable and is and is mainly mined from Lighting Ridge
